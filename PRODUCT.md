# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Communicative, good-natured dentists running active restorative practices — primarily in North America. They access the site from desktops in their offices and occasionally from tablets during patient visits. Their job: find a trusted lab partner who eliminates remakes, saves chair time, and protects their reputation. They are skeptical of outsourcing, sensitive to quality and compliance, and make purchasing decisions based on peer trust and risk reduction.

## Product Purpose

New-Karomi is a premium boutique dental laboratory where 3rd-generation heritage meets cutting-edge digital precision (46 years of family craftsmanship + 5-axis milling + full digital workflow). The website exists to attract and convert the right dentists into long-term B2B partners — not one-off clients. Success = a dentist submitting their first digital case or requesting a Starter Kit.

Motto: "Artistry in our heritage. Precision in our technology. Profitability in your practice."

## Brand Personality

Confident, warm, boundary-setting expert partner. The lab says "Apply for Partnership" not "Contact Us." It turns away rush jobs and misaligned clients. It positions its CDL/DAMAS certifications as "Risk Insurance" for the dentist's practice — framing quality as a financial and reputational guarantee, not a badge.

Three words: **Precise. Warm. Selective.**

## Anti-references

- Mass-production factory aesthetics (generic clinical white + teal)
- Cold tech-lab sterility (blue gradients, stock imagery of anonymous hands)
- Generic SaaS dental portals (navy + gold finance aesthetic)
- "AI-made" tells: gradient text, Inter for everything, purple-to-blue gradients, identical icon-tile grids
- Any design that could belong to a competitor who outsources overseas

## Design Principles

1. **Legacy as ROI** — Connect 46 years of heritage directly to the dentist's bottom line: zero remakes, saved chair time, protected reputation. Heritage is not nostalgia; it is reliability.
2. **Positive friction** — Use commitment-driven copy and CTAs to filter for the right partners. The site should feel exclusive without feeling cold.
3. **Show the craft** — High-magnification macro photography of restorations, real lab team faces, and visible technology (5-axis milling) over generic stock imagery. Authenticity builds trust with clinicians.
4. **Precision in every detail** — The design itself is proof of quality. No sloppy layouts, inconsistent spacing, or visual shortcuts. The site IS the portfolio.
5. **Context-aware experience** — Marketing surface: editorial, generous whitespace, asymmetric layouts. Client portal (future): high-density, data-first, 12-column grid. Never mix the registers on the same surface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum across all surfaces. Semantic color pairing required — never rely on color alone for meaning. Reduced motion via `prefers-reduced-motion`. Touch targets minimum 44px for tablet-using clinicians. Hebrew RTL support required (existing Hebrew copy for Israeli market).
